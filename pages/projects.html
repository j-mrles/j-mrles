Research: Investigation Of Large Language Models For Teaching Software Programming To Non-Programmers
More details here.

I investigated the efficacy of various large language models for teaching programming to non-programmers, developed large language training and tutorials for teaching software programming to non-programmers, and identified the strengths and weaknesses of large language models as a teaching tool and how they can be used to improve traditional teaching methods.

I founded Futuros en STEM because I believe that everyone deserves access to a quality STEM education, regardless of their background or circumstances. I saw firsthand the inequality in STEM education when I was a student, and I knew that I wanted to do something to change that.
